Quran's teacher at SRI Darul Bayan, January-July 2020.
At the request of my headmaster, I worked as a Quran teacher at SRI Darul Bayan while waiting for my SPM results. My schedule involved teaching Quran classes from
7 AM to 9 AM and then returning to school from 2 PM to 4 PM.
I taught students in Grade 1, who were a bit mischievous, but this experience greatly enhanced my skills in handling young children. Additionally, I conducted a tuition class for the Arabic language, as requested by the parents of my students. This tuition class took place only on Saturdays from 3 PM to 5 PM, with a daily salary of RM 50. For the Quran classes, I received a monthly salary of RM 150.

A sandwiches seller.
During every semester holiday, I assist my mom in selling various types of sandwiches from home. I handle the delivery of sandwiches directly to customers at their residences. Examples of sandwiches include sushi sandwiches, egg sandwiches, sardine sandwiches, and many more.

Assistant manager at Plastik Bira Jalan, a plastic shop, July-September 2022.
As an assistant manager at Plastik Bira Jalan, a shop specializing in plastic items, I undertook various responsibilities including cashier duties, stock ordering, overseeing cleanliness, and managing inventory. I worked six days a week and received a monthly salary of RM1300. Due to my academic pursuits, I could only commit to this role for two months.

A promoter of Fiffy brand at BabyWorld Paka, July-September 2023
I worked as a promoter for Fiffy brand baby items at BabyWorld, where I focused on promoting products, assembling hampers, and managing stock. From all my experiences, I particularly loved working at BabyWorld the most. The environment was vibrant with many friends, most of whom were girls. I worked four days a week, earning RM7.00 per hour, which totaled a monthly salary of RM1300.I deeply enjoyed the camaraderie and found the work fulfilling. Despite the physical demands, the friendships and supportive atmosphere made it a memorable experience for me.
